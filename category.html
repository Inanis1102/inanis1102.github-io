What is Fiction?

Fiction is any creative work, portraying individuals, events or places that are imaginary, or in ways that are imaginary. Fictional portrayals are thus inconsistent with history, fact or plausibility. In a traditional narrow sense, "fiction" refers to written narratives in prose - often referring specifically to novels and short stories
